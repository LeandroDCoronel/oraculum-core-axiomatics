# v5.0.0 — Operative Cost Layer & Mission System
2026-03-06

This version introduces the first **teleological layer** of Oraculum.

The system now goes beyond passive KPI tracking and begins to guide
the operator through structured missions representing **operative
costs of human development**.

## Added

- **Operative Cost menu (O)** in the main interface.
- Positive friction missions.
- Negative friction missions.
- Mission timers for monthly objectives.
- Initial missions:

### Mission 001 — Absolute Circadian Anchoring
Positive friction operating cost.

Milestones:
- Sleep before 21:00
- Wake up 05:00
- Morning run

### Mission 002 — Reorientation of Resource Flow
Negative friction operating cost.

Milestones:
- No weed
- No alcohol
- No compulsive scrolling
- Financial discipline

## Improved

- Daily **Oraculum Insight** system:
  - Detects strongest domain.
  - Detects weakest domain.
  - Flags critical weaknesses.

## Architecture

- Removal of **Regulation domain**
- Introduction of **Gallery domain**

Domains now:

A — Athletics  
B — Business  
T — Technology  
S — Science  
G — Gallery

This change begins **Cycle II** of Oraculum evolution.

Cycle I historical data preserved separately.
